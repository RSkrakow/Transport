## Root cause of Analiza vs Dyspozytorzy discrepancy (resolved 2026-06-10)

### Why numbers differed:
- 150 of 285 May routes had fracht=0 (invoices not yet issued)
- 149 of those had TMS marżaPerKm > 0 → estimated fracht (positive margin contribution)
- Analiza's `hideEstimated` toggle (when ON) excluded these 149 routes from KPI totals
- Dyspozytorzy has no such toggle → always included all routes
- Result: Analiza showed only invoiced routes (could be +/- depending on month),
  Dyspozytorzy showed all routes including TMS-estimated ones

1. Analiza toDateKey: added DD-MM-YYYY HH:MM handler (already in Dyspozytorzy)
